Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida) and 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee and milk as needed for dough
  • 1½ cups sugar, 1½ cups water, and cardamom pods

Recipe

  1. Mix the dry ingredients with ghee and milk to make a soft dough.
  2. Boil sugar, water, and cardamom to prepare the syrup.
  3. Make small balls and deep fry on low flame until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried jamuns in warm syrup and serve.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee and 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add condensed milk and milk powder.
  2. Stir continuously on low flame until the mixture thickens.
  3. Spread the mixture on a greased tray and add chopped nuts.
  4. Let it cool, cut into pieces, and serve.

Kalakand

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crumbled paneer
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp ghee and 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds for garnish

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add paneer and condensed milk.
  2. Cook on low flame while stirring until thick and grainy.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a greased tray and garnish with nuts.
  4. Let it cool, cut into pieces, and serve.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk and 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar and 4 cups water
  • 3 cups milk and 4 tbsp sugar for rabri
  • Cardamom powder and chopped nuts for garnish

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice, and prepare soft chenna.
  2. Make small balls and cook them in sugar syrup until spongy.
  3. Boil milk with sugar and cardamom to make rabri.
  4. Add the balls to rabri, chill, and serve with nuts.
